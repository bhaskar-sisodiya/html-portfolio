The Best Movies According to Bhaskar

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Intersteller

This is the most recent movie I watched. This is a science fiction movie directed by a famous film director Christopher Nolan.

Oppenheimer

This is another film directed by Christopher Nolan. This is a biopic of a great nuclear physicist J. Robert Oppenheimer.

The Kerela Story

This is a film inspired by the true incidents which took place in Kerela. This is the story of a hindu girl Shalini Unnikrishnan who was brainwashed, islamised, impregnated and sent to Syria to join ISIS.
